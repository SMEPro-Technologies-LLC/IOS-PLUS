The Problem

Every AI governance tool monitors what the AI did. None of them prove what the AI will do.

The pattern in AI enforcement actions is the same: the AI executes, compliance finds out after the fact, and the institution explains itself to regulators. SMEPro inverts the sequence. Governance executes first. AI executes second. Every decision is signed before it lands.
